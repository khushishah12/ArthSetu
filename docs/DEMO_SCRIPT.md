# Three-minute judge demo

1. Open the cinematic homepage and state the problem: financially responsible users can remain invisible when conventional history is thin.
2. Click "Get Your SetuScore" to enter the gamified questionnaire.
3. Complete the 6-phase, 20-question financial questionnaire (~2 minutes).
4. View the results: SetuScore, top 3 drivers, improvement missions, and investment plan.
5. Click "Go to Dashboard" to enter the command centre with your real data.
6. Review the score drivers, signal features, projection chart, and investment allocation.
7. Navigate to "Complete Assessment" to run a full risk-profile + investment assessment.
8. Navigate to "SetuInvest" to explore educational allocation and scenario paths.
9. Navigate to "Profile" to see your data sources and consent controls.
10. Finish on the disclaimer and responsible-use boundary.

## Key demo flow

- **Landing page** → "Get Your SetuScore"
- **Questionnaire** → 6 phases: Basics → Income → Spending → Bills → Digital → Investment
- **Results** → SetuScore + drivers + missions + plan
- **Dashboard** → Full command centre with your data
- **Assessment** → Risk profiling + full assessment
- **SetuInvest** → Educational investment scenarios
- **Profile** → Data sources and consent controls

## Responsible-use boundary

- SetuScore is not a credit-bureau score.
- The product does not make lending approval decisions.
- SetuInvest is educational and not regulated financial advice.
- Scenario values are illustrative and not promised returns.
- Training data is synthetic; user data stays in the browser (localStorage).
